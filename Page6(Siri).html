Siri

Siri is Apple virtual assistant for iOS, macOS, tvOS and watchOS devices that use voice recognition and is powered by artificial intelligence (AI).


Siri derived from a combination of multiple sources, projects and technologies. The defense advanced research projects agency (DARPA) funded the cognitive assistant that learns and organized (CALO) project, aiming to develop a personal assistant with machine learning technologies.


In compliance with your needs, it provides you with entertainment, including playing your favorite music or expanding your knowledge by answering questions. Siri is functioning is based on characteristic for AI constant machine learning. Its improvement is based mostly on collecting offline data directly from your device without violating your privacy regarding the online searching history.
