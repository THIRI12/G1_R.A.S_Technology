Alexa

Alexa is a virtual assistant designed by Amazon like Google Siri and much more. It has been designed for around 2 years and each year, it only gets smarter and smarter. As time has gone on, Amazon has added "Skills" to Alexa and made the virtual assistant far more powerful. And although Amazon says more people are using Alexa each year, many are still unfamiliar with the service.


Alexa also allow you to listen to radio stations, play trivia games, get lyrics support from Lyrical and much more. You can also use Alexa to get news updates, find out how your stocks are performing and, of course, control all facets of your smart home through apps such as SmartThings.
